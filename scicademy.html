Scicademy

Description

  • www.scicademy.org

  • Early 2015

Scicademy's goal was to create an open-access, open source, student research database for high school students so their research does not fade away into oblivion after a science fair. The value hypothesis was that high school students wanted to share their research online to benefit humanity.

User & Market Research

This was the first entrepreneurial venture we ever conducted. As a result of our inexperience, we did not conduct any user/market research or customer interviews, unwisely leaving us in the dark.
